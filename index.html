About Me

Hi! I’m Maria Fujisawa — a 17-year-old student, aspiring engineer, and researcher based in Tokyo, Japan. I’m currently exploring how virtual reality can shape the future of brain-machine interfaces, and I’ve built a few websites to support educational initiatives. I’m especially passionate about tackling the educational gap in hands-on STEM learning in Japan, and I’m working to create more accessible, engaging opportunities for students to explore science and technology.

I'm currently leading GMTC Japan and YSI Japan, two organizations aimed at bringing hands-on robotics and scientific learning to students across the country.
